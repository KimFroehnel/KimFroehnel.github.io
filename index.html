Biography

I am Kim Fröhnel, a Ph.D. candidate in Information Systems Management at the Information and Communication Management Lab at the Technical University of Berlin. My research interests include platform/sharing economy, trust-building and reputation. Besides my own research, I am also responsible for planning, elaboration, and execution of lecture-accompanying exercises courses (teaching tasks) and supervision of BSc and MSc theses.
